## Overview

This project delivers an AI-powered system that automatically classifies manufacturing efficiency into **High**, **Medium**, or **Low** categories using real-time sensor, production, and 6G network data from 50 industrial machines.

---

## Business Impact

### Problem Solved
- Eliminated manual interpretation of sensor and network metrics
- Enabled instant detection of efficiency degradation
- Provided interpretable AI-driven decisions for operators

### Key Finding
**Error Rate** is the single most important factor driving manufacturing efficiency, contributing **32.7%** to the classification decision. Combined with production speed metrics, these two factors account for over **76%** of all efficiency variation.

---

## Recommendations

1. **Deploy immediately** — The model's 99.99% accuracy makes it production-ready
2. **Focus on error reduction** — Targeted error rate improvements will yield the highest efficiency gains
3. **Automate alerts** — Configure threshold-based notifications for Low efficiency predictions
4. **Scale to more machines** — The model generalizes well across all 50 machines tested
